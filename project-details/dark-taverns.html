    Dark Taverns


    Introduction

    Dark Taverns, an immersive MMORPG game, takes players on an exhilarating journey through a mystical world filled with adventures, monsters, and legendary quests. Within this captivating gaming universe, players have the opportunity to not only partake in epic battles but also create a unique space of their own. Dark Taverns Builder, a remarkable sub-module of the game, empowers players to craft and customize their own taverns, transforming their digital assets into Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs).


    Key Features

    • Ownership: Dark Taverns Builder allows players to take ownership of their very own taverns within the game. These taverns are represented as NFTs, adding a layer of uniqueness and collectibility to the in-game assets.
    • Wallet Integration: The project seamlessly connects your digital wallet, enabling you to access and manage the NFT taverns you've acquired through the game.
    • Customization: Players can fully customize their taverns to reflect their personal taste and style. This includes decorating the interior, selecting furniture, and even adjusting the layout of the tavern.
    • Asset Library: The builder module retrieves a wide array of assets from an AWS bucket. This dynamic system ensures that players have access to a constantly evolving set of assets, allowing them to continually refresh and update their taverns.
    • Expandable Asset Repository: Dark Taverns Builder ensures that the asset library can be easily expanded by adding new assets to the AWS bucket. This enables a constantly growing selection of items and decor for players to choose from, enhancing their creative possibilities.
    • Built on Unity 3D: The project is built on the Unity 3D engine, providing a rich and immersive 3D gaming experience. With Unity's powerful physics engine, players can interact with their customized taverns in a dynamic and realistic way.

    Technology Stack

    • Unity 3D
    • AWS (Amazon Web Services)
    • Asset Bundles
    • Metamask
    • REST APIs
