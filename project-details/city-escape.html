    City Escape


    Introduction

    "City Escape" is an immersive augmented reality (AR) game that unfolds in a rich urban landscape. As a unique multiplayer experience, it leverages Firebase Realtime Database to allow players to collaborate as detectives. Their mission: investigate crime scenes scattered throughout the city. When a player reaches a marker on the map, an AR scene opens, enabling them to interact with the environment, collect evidence, and collectively uncover the secrets of the city.


    Key Features

    • Multiplayer Detective Adventure: In "City Escape," players team up as detectives in an online world. They work together to explore the city, analyze crime scenes, and gather evidence to solve mysteries.
    • Realtime Database with Firebase: The game's multiplayer interactions and progress are powered by Firebase Realtime Database. This feature ensures that players can collaborate seamlessly, sharing discoveries and progress in real-time.
    • Augmented Reality Exploration: The game offers an augmented reality experience, providing players with a captivating and immersive way to explore crime scenes. AR scenes allow players to interact with the environment and uncover clues.
    • Interactive Crime Scenes: Each marker on the map represents a crime scene that players must investigate. The AR scenes are interactive, allowing players to collect evidence, solve puzzles, and piece together the narrative.
    • Collaborative Gameplay: "City Escape" emphasizes teamwork, as players must work together to analyze the evidence and solve crimes. This cooperative aspect adds a layer of depth to the gameplay and encourages communication and collaboration.

    Technology Stack

    • Unity 2D
    • Unity AR
    • Firebase Realtime Database
    • Online Maps v3

    Platforms
